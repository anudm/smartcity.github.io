Agriculture Case Study

Agriculture Case Study

Agriculture plays a crucial role in supporting sustainable urban development as outlined in SDG Goal 11. By integrating agricultural practices into urban and peri-urban areas, it helps create green spaces, reduce food insecurity, and improve environmental quality.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Urban Agriculture and Planning (Target 11.3):
    • Enhances land use efficiency.
    • Links land consumption with population growth (Indicator 11.3.1).
  • Rural-Urban Connectivity (Target 11.A):
    • Promotes sustainable development by connecting urban areas with rural agricultural regions.
    • Encourages cities to adopt regional plans that include agriculture (Indicator 11.A.1).

Case Highlights:

  • Visits to Agricultural Research Centers, Farms, and Market Yards demonstrate efforts to monitor and optimize agricultural operations.
  • Integration of Dijkstra's Algorithm in planning travel routes improves logistics and efficiency in monitoring these sites.
  • The study emphasizes the role of agriculture in enhancing urban sustainability and fostering rural-urban linkages.

This case study illustrates how urban agriculture aligns with global sustainability goals and supports resilient city planning.
